Data Collection and Processing

We collect:
  • From calendars: event titles, start/end times, participant information
  • From documents: file names, creation/modification dates, author information
  • From emails: sender, recipient, subject line, timestamps
  • From voice messages: timestamps and activity information extracted via keyword analysis

Access is granted through OAuth connections with Google and Microsoft, after you provide explicit consent. Voice messages are processed only when voluntarily shared through WhatsApp.

System Architecture and Integration

Our system follows four main stages:
  1. User Authentication - You authenticate through Google or Microsoft OAuth
  2. Metadata Collection - We collect metadata from your connected services
  3. Metadata Processing - Our AI processes this information
  4. Timesheet Presentation - You receive a pre-filled timesheet for review

Contera integrates with:
  • Google Workspace (Calendar and Drive)
  • Microsoft Office 365 (Outlook and OneDrive)
  • WhatsApp (for voice message metadata)

You can send voice messages to our WhatsApp bot describing your activities. The system extracts only the metadata (like timestamps and activity descriptions) from these messages to supplement your timesheet data. We use the WhatsApp API to securely process these messages.

You can request deletion of your data at any time through the "Data Deletion" section in the platform. All your personal metadata will be permanently deleted, though anonymized statistical data may be retained.

We process deletion requests promptly, and your data will be removed from our active systems. You'll receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

If you delete your historical data, you can still continue using the service, which will begin collecting new metadata from that point forward (if you maintain your consent).
